Turning Lines into Stories

My Journey

I am a self-taught drawer based in Kolhapur, Maharashtra. My journey into art began unexpectedly, sparked by a pivotal moment of introspection.

My Process & Inspiration

My work is primarily focused on pencil and charcoal, as I am fascinated by the infinite range of emotions that can be conveyed through monochrome shades and textures. I find inspiration in the everydayâ€”the subtle expressions on a person's face, the play of light and shadow on old architecture, and the raw beauty of nature. Each piece is a meditation, a slow and deliberate process of building layers to bring an idea to life.

Artist Statement

Through my art, I aim to capture and explore the quiet narratives that exist within a single moment. My goal is to create images that are not just seen but felt, inviting the viewer to pause and connect with the story held within the lines.
